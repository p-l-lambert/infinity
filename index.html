ABOUT Take Me To...

Take Me To... was founded by Team Infinity at Code First: Girls. It stemmed from our own holiday experiences, our attempts to discover entire new cities in just a few days.

Tour books and other travel websites give us unlimited options of what to see, what to do and where to eat when on holiday. It can take days just to filter through all the recommendations - so when you only have a couple, you want to spend all your time exploring and doing things you love. Take Me To... gives you tried and tested day plans that you can use in whatever way you want, as guides to what you can realistically fit in one day, and how you can best order your activities (and food stops!)

"Days off work or studying are precious. Our aim is to help you make the most of your time, so you can both explore new places and relax whilst doing so."

Team Infinity

Early Morning

Watch the sun rise over London's famous city skyline from Alexandra Palace's beautiful landscaped gardens before heading into the city


Morning

Visit the Thames river's most famous path along the South Bank, where you can hop on the London Eye for another spectacular view of the city or drop into one of the many riverside attractions like the London Aquarium and the South Bank Centre


Early Afternoon

Just a short walk over Westminster bridge you can visit the elegant Houses of Parliament, steeped in history and tradition, plus Westminster Abbey and Big Ben is all within a moments walk of one another


Afternoon

A trip to London isn't complete without a walk up Pall Mall, through St James' Park to the most famous palace in the world, Buckingham Palace


Evening

From the bright lights of Piccadilly Circus and it's range of shops to the arts and culture of the National Gallery in Trafalgar Square. Buy some tickets to one of London's famous theatre's and go along to a pre-theatre meal in the West End

Early Morning

Go for breakfast at CookShop on 10th - the fluffiest pancakes you'll have here! The Huevos Rancheros also come (very) highly recommended as does the cold brew.


Morning

Walk the Highline - walk off your breakfast on New York's raised garden on the old central railroad, with 2.33km of great views across midtown, art and flowers.


Lunchtime

Chelsea - grab a snack from the market (Fat Witch Bakery does amazing brownies) and wind back uptown towards 34th Street. Go via Union Square or Madison Square Park if you fancy some greenery or a moment to sit down and watch the world go by.


Afternoon

Empire State Building - get in the queue so you can watch the sun set over the city with one of the best views in town. It's world famous for a reason, the view is breathtaking!


Evening

Head to local pizza joint Little Italy for a slice of authentic NYC pizza for dinner. You can't go wrong! If you can, try and catch some culture afterwards - a Broadway show or jazz bar (such as the famous Birdland) are great ways to end the day!

Early Morning

Rialto Market, Walking Tour Kick start your morning in Venice like the locals by visiting the historic Rialto Market. Sprawling on the western banks of the Grand Canal, this market is a hive of activity.


Morning

Basilica di San Marco, Church After the market tour, walk to St. Mark's Square and visit Basilica di San Marco , try to visit nice and early in the day to escpae the crowd


Lunchtime

Doge's Palace, Museum Right next door to the Basilica is the Doge's Palace, the seat of political and administrative power of the Venitian Republic from its birth to its end.


Afternoon

Dorsoduro, Walking Tour After strolling around St. Mark's Square, make your way to a quiter and quainter part of the city called Dorsoduro.


Evening

Gondola Ride, Watercraft. Rest your legs and enjoy the most quintessentially Venetian affair. The ideal time for a gondola ride would be early evening, around sunset.

Early Morning

Start walking on the Bol'shoy Moskvoretskiy Most with the best view on the Kremlin


Morning

Go inside Park Zaryad'ye straigh after the bridge. Here you can relax from the city and try traditional Russian cuisine with a fair price. Also, don't forget to have a look on the Concert Hall architecture.


Lunchtime

Evening
